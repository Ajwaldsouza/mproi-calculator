Yield model: Y(DLI) = Ymax × (1 − e−k·DLI). Parameters fitted from published yield-DLI data (Pennisi et al., 2020) using nonlinear least squares.

Energy model: Total energy per mol DLI = lighting + HVAC cooling. HVAC multiplier (α) calculated as 1 + (1 − ηLED) / COP, using COP values from Graamans et al. (2018) for each climate zone.

Profit: Revenue (yield × selling price) minus energy cost (lighting + cooling) per crop cycle, expressed per m² per day. Variable costs (seeds, nutrients, labour) are excluded — the metric isolates the light–energy decision.

Limitations: Does not model cycle-time effects on annual throughput, time-of-use electricity tariffs, CO&sub2; supplementation interactions, or tip-burn / quality losses at high DLI.
